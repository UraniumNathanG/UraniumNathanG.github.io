Pokemon GO

Pokemon Go Intro

Almost Everyone from almost every country is crazy about this new pokemon game, Pokemon GO. Go around the world, catching pokemon and fighting gyms with your high CP (Combat Power) Pokemon and taking over gyms. This is some tips that might(maybe not) help you.

E.X.P Stratergy

One of the stratergys is the exp stratergy. You have to be very patient if you want to succeed. First you have to collect a lot of candy from catching pokemon or hatching eggs and make sure you have many pokemon that can evolve. Then use a lucky egg and quickly evolve your pokemon. For each new pokemon you evolve, you get 2000exp, if used lucky eg. If you already have the evolved pokemon, the you get 1000exp if you used lucky egg. If you are done evolving, go outside to spin pokestops and catch more pokemon before the time of the lucky egg runs out.

Pokemon GO Easter Eggs

Pokemon GO has some "easter eggs". The first thing is eevee evolutions. If you want your eevee to evolve into specific jolteon, flareon or vaporeon, you will have to nickname your eevee. If you want a vaporeon, nickname eevee "Rainer", for jolteon, nickname it "sparky" and for flareon, nickname it "Pyro". Before evolving turn off your game, and then enter it again. The second easter egg is how to get me, pikachu as a starter. After you customize youself, you will see charmander, bulbasaur and squirtle. Instead, ignore them and walk as far as possible, you might find pikachu.

And That's It

Good Luck with your adventure.
