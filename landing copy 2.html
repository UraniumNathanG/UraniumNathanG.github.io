Pokemon TCG Tips

First tip, don't put all ex and break cards in your deck. If you put your deck with all ex cards, some pokemon cards have immune to ex cards attacks, therfore, you are of course going to lose, bild your deck, not only with strong cards, but cards you can stratergize with and also build your deck with enough energy cards and trainer cards.
