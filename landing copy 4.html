Pokemon Sun and Moon

Pokemon Sun and Moon Intro

Pokemon Sun and Moon is finally coming out on November 18, 2016 and they added new pokemon and other evoultions. Pokemon Sun and Moon is a 3DS game, like Pokemon Omega Ruby, and you go around the new Alohla Region to catch pokemon, battle gyms and beat the pokemon league. Pokemon Z was going to come out, but due to Pokemon Sun and Moon, they canceled it and let Sun and Moon take over. If you are a Pokemon fan, you should play this game. It has better graphics and new features. If you read on, you will see the new features of sun and moon.

Pokemon Sun and Moon New Features and New Pokemon

Pokemon Sun and Moon trailers show a lot of new features and pokemon. Firstly, there are new pokemon that sound and look weird, but they are still new pokemon. The starters are called Litten(Fire), Poppilo(Water) and Rowlet(Grass). Pikipek and Yungoos are like the "Pidgey and Rattata" of the game. The new legendary pokemon are called Solgaleo(Sun) and Lunala(Moon). One of the new features are that you can see your opponent when battling. You can also ride a Stoutland and fly on a Charizard. There is a new kind of evoulution called Alola Evolution, changing 5 pokemon. Exeggcutor's neck is longer and Vulpix, Ninetailes, Sandshrew and Sandslash Ice type.
